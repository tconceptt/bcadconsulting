# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Three audiences, in order of business value:

1. **Development agencies & program funders** (GIZ, USAID, ITC, World Bank, UN Women, USADF): procurement officers and program managers vetting a local implementation partner. They arrive skeptical and time-poor; they need evidence of institutional weight and delivery history.
2. **Ethiopian & African enterprises** needing consulting, HR/BPO, or solar energy: owners and executives who want a firm that understands their market, not an airport-lounge consultancy.
3. **Aspiring entrepreneurs** considering the "Building a Purpose-Driven Business" training: they need to feel inspired and trust the program enough to pay and register.

## Product Purpose

Marketing site for BCaD Consulting Management PLC (Addis Ababa, est. 1998) — management consulting, entrepreneurship training, BPO/HR services, renewable energy (Ketir Solar), and dietetic counselling (Bekidmia). The site must win institutional trust from funders while inspiring entrepreneurs to register for training. Success = contact-form inquiries from organizations and training registrations.

## Brand Personality

**Established. Enterprising. Warm-authoritative.** A 27-year-old firm that has delivered the EU's, USADF's, and ITC's hardest field programs — but whose heart is the individual Ethiopian entrepreneur. Confident without being cold; inspiring without being naive. The tagline is "Spurring Innovation and Entrepreneurship" and the logo's key is the brand's core metaphor: unlocking potential.

Brand standards are fixed and committed (see brand manual): Primary Blue #1E5AA8, Deep Navy #0F2C4C, Innovation Gold #DAA857, Entrepreneurship Teal #1FA7A0, Steel Gray #A7A9AC, Soft Gray #F5F6F8. Montserrat for headlines, Source Sans 3 for body. The key element may be used as a subtle background pattern at 5–10% opacity. Line-style icons.

## Anti-references

- **Seed-stage SaaS**: no gradient buttons, floating app screenshots, playful blob illustrations. This firm predates the companies that invented that look.
- **NGO donor-report template**: no smiling-beneficiary hero + impact-stat-soup + pale-blue-everything. BCaD is a commercial firm that delivers development work, not a charity.
- **Bureaucratic bank/government site**: corporate must not mean dated, cold, or beige.

## Design Principles

1. **The key is the story.** The brand metaphor (unlocking enterprise) should be felt in layout, copy, and motif — not just pasted as a logo.
2. **Evidence over adjectives.** Named programs, named funders, real years. The track record is the sales pitch.
3. **Ethiopia is the setting, not a costume.** Imagery of real commerce and place, treated in the brand's own palette; no safari-brochure exoticism and no cultural-flag palettes.
4. **Institutional weight, entrepreneurial pulse.** Deep-navy gravity for the firm; teal and gold energy for the people it serves.
5. **One idea per fold.** Long scroll, deliberate pacing, generous type.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG 2.1 AA. Body text ≥4.5:1 contrast, large text ≥3:1. Full `prefers-reduced-motion` alternatives (already enforced globally in `app/globals.css`). Semantic landmarks and real heading hierarchy. Many users are on mid-range Android devices over slow connections in Ethiopia — keep pages light, self-host fonts via next/font, optimize images.
